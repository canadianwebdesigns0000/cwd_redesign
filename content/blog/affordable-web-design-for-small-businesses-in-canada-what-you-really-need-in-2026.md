<h2 class="wp-block-heading">What Affordable Web Design Should Include (Not Cheap Design)</h2>

<p>Affordable does not mean cutting corners. Professional <strong>web design services in Canada</strong> should still include essential features that support growth.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Mobile‑First Design</h3>

<p>More than half of website traffic comes from mobile devices. Your site must look and function perfectly on phones and tablets.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">SEO‑Ready Structure</h3>

<p>A properly structured website helps search engines understand your content. This includes:</p>

<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Clean URLs</li>

<li>Proper heading hierarchy</li>

<li>Optimized images</li>

<li>Fast page speed</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Fast Loading Speed</h3>

<p>Slow websites lose visitors and rankings. Affordable web design should still prioritize performance optimization.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Secure and Reliable Platform</h3>

<p>SSL certificates, secure hosting, and regular updates protect both your business and your customers.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Easy Content Management</h3>

<p>Using platforms like WordPress allows business owners to update content without technical expertise.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Common Mistakes Small Businesses Make When Choosing a Web Designer</h2>

<p>Many Canadian small businesses struggle online because of poor decisions made early in the web design process.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Choosing Price Over Value</h3>

<p>The cheapest option often leads to redesigns later. Investing slightly more upfront usually saves money long‑term.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Ignoring SEO From the Start</h3>

<p>A website without SEO is like opening a store with no signage. Design and SEO should work together.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">No Local Market Understanding</h3>

<p>Designers unfamiliar with the Canadian market may overlook regional preferences, compliance requirements, or local competition.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Lack of Ongoing Support</h3>

<p>Websites require maintenance. Without support, performance and security can quickly decline.</p>
