<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-creating-website-designs-with-these-essential-elements">Creating website designs with these essential elements.</h2>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-1-color-palette">1. Color Palette</h3>

<p>The color palette is the most common element in all aspects, yet useful in styling <a href="https://www.bluleadz.com/blog/bid/68850/5-elements-of-a-modern-website-design">features</a>. Without adding a color palette to your website designs, it often brings dull and boring looks, right? Choosing the right palette will depend on the theme, style, or color of the logo brand.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-2-navigation-menu">2. Navigation Menu</h3>

<p>Creating an easy and straightforward navigation menu for your website designs. Will help the users distinguish the specific pages they want to explore. Avoid an overloaded list of the navigational menu as possible to prevent visitors from getting lost or frustrated. The nav menu often occurs on the top and bottom parts of the site.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-3-content">3. Content</h3>

<p>The website's content usually contains information about the company, service offered, and contact details. Not only for the website designs but also for the usability of search engine optimization. Creating efficient content must be engaging, have appropriate typography, excellent readability, and an organized format.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-4-call-to-action">4. Call-to-action</h3>

<p>It is necessary to provide a call-to-action to your website to make it easier for the users. To reach your customer service for further queries or to book an appointment. If you visit different homepages, you will notice that most website designs add buttons, contact information, or sign-up form. When placing a call-to-action display, put it in the center and make sure it will be visible.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-5-whitespaces">5. Whitespaces</h3>

<p>Don't underestimate the blank spaces. Even though it's invisible, it helps website designs establish clear, concise, and organized formats of other elements. Disregarding the usage of white margins on formatting the design elements will produce untidy looks and decrease your content's readability. Every time you are using spaces, ensuring the uniformity of height is vital for improving its presentable appearance.</p>

<p>Read also: <a href="https://canadianwebdesigns.ca/website-designs-using-social-proofing-techniques/">Website Designs: Using Social Proofing Techniques</a></p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-6-images-and-videos">6. Images and videos</h3>

<p>Including visual imagery on your <a href="https://canadianwebdesigns.ca/online-presence-how-this-works/">website designs</a> such as graphic images, logos, pictures, and videos can draw visitors' attention. Presenting photos about the services and your company also helps them to visualize it. In ordinary cases, we often use stock photos instead of the actual ones. Overloaded usage of stock images will be more likely to end up look like unreliable.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-7-mobile-compatibility">7. Mobile compatibility</h3>

<p>Keep in mind that nowadays, most users browse through their smartphones rather than on computers. Meaning to say, it is vital to optimizing your website designs in any screen size. Make sure that all elements will appear on the mobile version of your site. An innovative website design is worthless if it's not compatible with mobile and tablets.</p>
