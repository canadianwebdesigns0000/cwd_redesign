<h2 class="wp-block-heading">The Hidden Cost of Neglecting Website Maintenance</h2>

<p>Many business owners build a website and forget it — but an unmaintained site quickly falls behind competitors.</p>

<p>Here’s what happens without proper upkeep:</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Slow Load Speeds</strong></h3>

<p>Plugins, themes, and unused scripts accumulate over time, slowing your site down and hurting SEO.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Security Vulnerabilities</strong></h3>

<p>Outdated software invites malware, spam, and data breaches — all of which damage your rankings and reputation.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Broken Pages &amp; Errors</strong></h3>

<p>404 errors, outdated links, and display issues push users away and increase bounce rates.</p>

<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Drop in Search Visibility</strong></h3>

<p>Google prioritizes fresh, healthy, and active websites. Neglect can cause ranking declines.</p>

<p>A strong website needs continuous care — and that’s where a professional maintenance strategy becomes valuable.</p>
