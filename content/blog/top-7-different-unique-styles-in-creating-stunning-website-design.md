<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-1-illustrative-website-designs"><strong>1. Illustrative website designs</strong></h2>

<p>This type of web design style consists of visualized features. Such as colorful graphics, decorative cartoons, and responsive animations in presenting the information. In making illustrative online creations, it requires a lot of time and effort to meet the targeted format. It is often used by skilled web developers in establishing their digital portfolios.</p>

<p>When you want your website with a simplified and minimal design, this style is suitable for you. Unlike illustrative web designs. <p>The common misconception about realistic web designs is the usage of original real-life videos or photos. <p>Everybody loves to see unique and custom graphics on websites with 3D effects. An isometric projection generally uses three-dimensional graphical visualizations. <h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-7-parallax-website-designs"><strong>7. Parallax website designs</strong></h2>

<p>A modernized website design which usually composed of parallax scrolling. That creates a visual illusion for the visitors. For most websites with parallax scrolling transitions, their background elements move at a slower rate than foreground content. Perfectly used for generating lead visitors with the proper placement of the call-to-action button.</p>
