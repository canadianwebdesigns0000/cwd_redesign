<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-1-introducing-the-company-s-background"><strong>1. Introducing the company’s background</strong></h2>

<p>Display necessary information about your company and how does it work. State the number of experiences you have in the home service industry. You can also provide your mission, vision, and values to show your stepping stone goal. People would like to know if your business is verifiable.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-2-presenting-the-offered-services-or-products"><strong>2. Presenting the offered services or products</strong></h2>

<p>Most digital visitors usually search for services or products that will fit their needs. In professional <a href="https://canadianwebdesigns.ca/minimalist-website-design/">website designs</a>, they often write descriptions along with their offers. Inform your clients by introducing your services and the specific areas where you can assist them.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-3-providing-the-address-and-contact-information"><strong>3. Providing the address and contact information</strong></h2>

<p>Contact information is the most important details about your business. In some cases, they don’t want to show their physical address for security reasons. It’s optional whether you want to display it or not. Additionally, put a call-to-action button where they can take an action immediately.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-4-displaying-original-images-and-videos"><strong>4. Displaying original images and videos</strong></h2>

<p>Take advantage of using original photos and videos on your website. Not only for aesthetic purposes but also provide accurate visual details relating to your business. Make sure to optimize with a high-quality resolution to impress your perspectives at very first glance.</p>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-5-encouraging-the-clients-to-leave-a-review"><strong>5. Encouraging the clients to leave a review</strong></h2>

<p>Another way to catch the attention of perspectives leads is by featuring the testimonials of your clients. It’s still considered an efficient online marketing strategy to put into your website design. Positive feedbacks and five-star ratings from Yelp, Google reviews, or others will likely impact one’s purchasing decision.</p>
