<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for Your Website</h2>

<p>To build immediate trust and address common customer concerns, your website should include a clear FAQ section with answers to questions like these:</p>

<ol start="1" class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Are you licensed, bonded, and insured?</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><em>Answer should clearly state your licensing status, provide license numbers, and explain the coverage your insurance/bonding offers to protect the client.</em></li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>What is your service area, and is there a travel charge?</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><em>Answer should specify the cities/regions you serve and outline any policies regarding travel fees for customers outside the core service zone.</em></li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>What payment methods do you accept, and what is your payment schedule?</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><em>Answer should list accepted payment types (e.g., credit card, e-transfer, cheque) and clearly describe when payments are due (e.g., deposit required, milestone payments, payment on completion).</em></li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>Do you offer a warranty on your work?</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><em>Answer should detail the length and scope of your labour warranty and clearly state how manufacturer warranties on materials are handled.</em></li>
</ul>
</li>

<li><strong>How long does it take to get an estimate or schedule a project?</strong>
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><em>Answer should provide a typical timeline for consultation, estimate delivery, and how far out your current project schedule is booked.</em></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
