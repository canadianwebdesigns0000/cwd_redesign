<p>Any marketing effort involving an electronic device or the internet is referred to as digital marketing. Businesses use digital platforms such as search engines, social media, email, and other websites to interact with present and potential consumers.</p>

<p>With digital marketing, you can reach a larger audience than you could with traditional marketing, and you can target the prospects most likely to buy your product or service. It's much less expensive than traditional advertising, and you can monitor results daily and make changes as needed.</p>

<p>Print advertising, phone calls, and physical marketing are examples of traditional marketing, whereas digital marketing is done electronically and online. This means that businesses have many more ways to communicate with customers, including email, video, social media, and search engines.</p>
